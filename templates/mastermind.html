Goal
  • Correctly guess the hidden pattern of colors with a limited number of guesses.
Directions
  • Click a color to select it, then click a spot in guess row to set a color in your guess sequence.
  • The mystery pattern has no duplicates.
  • Click the "Check" button to evaluate your guess.
  • Red and white pips will explain how accurate your guess was.
  • Red: color that was correctly placed in the sequence. White: color that is present, but in the the incorrect spot.
  • Determining which colors in your guess correspond to which pip is up to YOU to figure out.
  • Guess the sequence exactly before you run out of opportunities. If you run out of guesses, you LOSE. Use your guesses wisely!
